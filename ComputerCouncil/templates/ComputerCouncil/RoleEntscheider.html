Your role
A random procedure has predetermined whether each participant is part of a pair (Player X and an Algorithm) or an individual decision-maker (Player Z).
This random procedure assigned you to be
Player X.
The assignment as Player X or Player Z will remain the same throughout the session.
In this experiment, there will be 10 decision making rounds. In each round, a pair consisting of a Player X (=you) and the Algorithm will make decisions, which, together with the decision of Player Z, determine each participantâ€™s payoff in a given round.
In each of the 10 decision making rounds, you and a Player Z-participant will be randomly grouped. The identity of the other participant can change from round to round.
You will not learn of the identity of the other participant. No other participant will learn of your identity.
Your decision and others' decisions
You and the Algorithm receive identical information about the payoffs associated with six different Projects (Projects A-F).
The six Projects A-F may differ regarding the payoffs they offer to you and to Player Z.
The decision which Project is implemented is made by Player Z.
The only information that Player Z will have for making the decision which Project to implement is the wording of a message that you send to him or her.
The messages that you can send to Player Z read:
  • Message A: "Project A will earn you most."
  • Message B: "Project B will earn you most."
  • Message C: "Project C will earn you most."
  • Message D: "Project D will earn you most."
  • Message E: "Project E will earn you most."
  • Message F: "Project F will earn you most."
Your role: After seeing the information about Projects A-F, the Algorithm makes a recommendation to you which message you should send to Player Z.
After receiving the Algorithm's recommendation, you decide which message is sent to Player Z.
After receiving the message that you have sent, Player Z decides which of the six Projects is implemented. Note again that the Message you send is the only information that Player Z will have.
